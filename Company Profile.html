community engagements

Advancement of Youth through community outreach programme. Youth desk and community centres to be implemented across the country so that people can table and discuss issues that hinders success. Having campaign to address issues affecting communities such as Drug and Substance abuse awareness campaign and Gender based violence awareness campaign to help better the lives and empower women.

Attending to basic needs of disadvantage young girls and boys in helping them with sanitary towels and to attend to issues which hinders their performance in school and other external issues. Organisation is also looking forward to bringing career exhibition to pupils from disadvantaged communities to their convenient points to avoid lack of career guidance due to issues associated with financial and transport constraints.
